King Fireplace Suite

Designed as a private retreat within the hotel, this suite pairs generous proportions with warm materials — wide-plank floors, layered textiles, and soft light from two exposures. The living area flows naturally toward the hearth, where an evening fire turns the Rockies into a backdrop instead of a schedule.

A discreet wet bar and dining nook make longer stays effortless, while the bedroom remains deliberately calm: king bed, blackout layers, and quiet hardware so nothing competes with the view or the silence after dark.
