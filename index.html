Juniper Bistro

A relaxed dining room where the Rockies meet the plate — honest cooking, warm service, and a wine list chosen for long evenings after the trails.

Tucked into the hotel, Juniper Bistro is our answer to mountain appetite: seasonal menus built from Alberta producers, classic technique, and the occasional surprise from the chargrill. Breakfast fuels early hikes; dinner slows down with shared plates, mains, and desserts you will talk about on the gondola the next day.

  • Hours — Breakfast daily 7–11 · Dinner Wed–Sun 5–10
  • Style — Casual refinement; families and couples welcome
  • Dietary — Vegetarian options; ask your server about allergies

Welcome to Banff and the Bow Valley

There is deep and enduring memory of this valley held by Ktunaxa, Secwepemc, Métis, Dene & Tsu T’ina, Mountain Cree, Siksika & Blackfoot, and Stoney Nakoda peoples. We acknowledge Treaty Seven (1877) and the Treaty of 1895 between the salmon and buffalo peoples. We are grateful to live here with all our relations.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can often be arranged — just let us know when you book and we'll do our best to accommodate.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours or no-shows are subject to a one-night charge. Special rates and packages may have different terms — these will be clearly noted at the time of booking.

Is breakfast included with my stay?

Standard room rates are room-only. However, our Bistro serves breakfast daily from 7:00 AM, and bed-and-breakfast packages are available when booking. We recommend the Bistro's seasonal morning menu — guests love it.

Is parking available at Juniper?

Yes — complimentary outdoor parking is available on-site for all hotel guests. Electric vehicle charging stations are also available; please request one at the time of booking to ensure availability.

How far is Juniper from downtown Banff?

We're perched on the slopes of Mount Norquay, approximately 1.5 km from the centre of Banff — about a 5-minute drive or a scenic 20-minute walk along Banff Avenue. A complimentary shuttle into town runs twice daily.

Are pets welcome at the hotel?

We welcome well-behaved dogs in select ground-floor rooms for a small nightly fee. Please let us know at the time of booking so we can prepare your room. Cats and other animals are not accommodated at this time.
