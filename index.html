Juniper Hotel & Bistro

Juniper Bistro

A relaxed dining room where the Rockies meet the plate — honest cooking, warm service, and a wine list chosen for long evenings after the trails.

Tucked into the hotel, Juniper Bistro is our answer to mountain appetite: seasonal menus built from Alberta producers, classic technique, and the occasional surprise from the chargrill. Breakfast fuels early hikes; dinner slows down with shared plates, mains, and desserts you will talk about on the gondola the next day.

  • ◇ Hours — Breakfast daily 7–11 · Dinner Wed–Sun 5–10
  • ◇ Style — Casual refinement; families and couples welcome
  • ◇ Dietary — Vegetarian options; ask your server about allergies
