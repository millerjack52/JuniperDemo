Bistro details

Casual refinement at Banff's edge

Set inside the Juniper Hotel, our dining room pairs relaxed energy with ingredients from Alberta farms and producers. Breakfast is bright and nourishing, dinner is unhurried and shareable, and every table opens onto mountain light.

The kitchen balances comfort and craft: house-baked breads, charcoal-grilled proteins, and seasonal produce prepared simply so the landscape still leads.
